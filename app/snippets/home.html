The Proposal - His Perspective

The day of the proposal was a normal, but really happy day for us. We goofed around, walked around Berry's campus, and had a great day. While talking at Rachael's dorm room, I initiated a re-occuring, sweet inside joke between us. She used to wear another ring on her ring finger, and I used to take a look at it and say: "I really want a different ring on that finger". After I intiated that conversation that night, it just felt so right to be talking about marraige, and I felt deep in my heart that this is who I wanted to spend my life with, for like the hundredth time. So, I asked her if she could help me with something, and when we were in the middle of her dorm room, I asked her I she would help me to put a different, shinier ring on her finger. More importantly though, if she would like to be my wife.

The Proposal - Her Perspective

I was not expecting a proposal at all on that particular day. I knew it was coming sometime. I knew he had a ring and a plan, and I had been trying to get him to give me hints for months! But on that weekend, when he came to visit me at Berry, I was under the impression that the ring was being sized at a jeweler somewhere. We had had a wonderful time together that day, and that evening we were hanging out in my dorm room when somehow we got back on the topic of how much we wanted to marry each other. Before I knew it, he was down on one knee and popping the question! I couldn't have been happier or more surprised!
